Plants!

I've always had a "green thumb", but as I've learned what plants work best with my lifestyle & skills, instead of aesthetics, it's gotten much easier!

I've included basic information about each, from basic name to keeping info.

This is Maranta leuconeura. I've had this plant about two years & she's exploded growth. I've heard that any "Prayer Plant" is typically hard to keep, but I've had nothing put great progress. They enjoy high humidity & lots of shade.
This is a Dracaena marginata colorama (a mouthful right?), or Dragon Tree for short. This specific plant was a cutting from my father's plant & if you didn't know, propogated cuttings are genetic clones of the mother plant. To my partners' dismay, I really enjoy tree-keeping.
