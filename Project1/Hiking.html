Exploring the Outdoors

Humpback Mountain, VA, USA
  • On a cloudy day, Humpback Rock doesn't have much to offer.
  • The peak reaches ~3100ft, basically a right angle ascent at some points.
  • This was the highest Peak I've been to, with little reward as it was pouring rain. I had to take my glasses off to ensure I was seeing nothing.
Lake Michigan, MI, USA
  • Moving down to a lower point, we have an autumn Lake Michigan sunset.
  • This exact photo was taken off the road from Muskegon State Park. Michigan isn't everyone's top vacation destination, but I was pleasantly surprised at how enjoyable it was.
  • I'm not sure if it's the calm waves that swam in or the crisp lakeside air, but the ambiance is nowhere near comparable to the lakes & reservoirs of Maryland. We didn't get to access parks by foot b/c of unforseen circumstances, but even from the parking lot it was stunning.
