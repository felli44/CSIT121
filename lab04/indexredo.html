Japanese-Style Chicken Curry

A savory & hearty curry guaranteed to keep your belly full on frigid nights. This is a recipe I found on Pinterest & have made for years, with modifications - have fun with it!

Completed Meal

Ingredients

  • 6 Chicken Thighs; boneless + skinless
  • 2 Medium Sliced Carrots
  • 4 Diced Garlic Cloves; or as many as your heart desires
  • 1 Large Onion, Halved & Diced.
  • 2 Large Diced Potatoes; peeled or unpeeled.
  • 1 TBSP Honey
  • 2 TBSP Soy Sauce
  • 2 TBSP Curry Powder; mild, or spicy if you're bold
  • 2 TBSP Flour, sifted
  • 1 TSP Garam Masala
  • 2 Cups Chicken Stock
  • 2 TBSP Vegetable Oil; the recipe referenced calls for Veg. but I use Olive or Avocado oils
  • Rice to serve; rinsed

Here's How to Make It:

  1. Heat oil until fragrant in a medium saucepan; add Chicken.
  2. Cook chicken until browned completely; set aside & deglaze pan by adding more oil; use Medium heat.
  3. Add Onions & Garlic; cook until softened, around 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add Flour, Curry Powder & Garam Masala. Cook for 1-2 minutes, then gradually add the Chicken Stock.
  5. Once combined, add Honey & Soy Sauce
  6. Add previously browned Chicken, Carrots, & Potatoes.
  7. Slowly bring to an even boil & simmer for 15-20 minutes.
